Do you already or will you receive any income outside of a salary from an employer?

You don't need to register as a business if:
  1. You are an employee of a company and only receive a W2 to report your income.
  2. You make your income through the payment of dividends from corporate shares you hold or by interest paid from passive investments.
You do need to register as a business if:
  1. You will work as an independent contractor and receive one or more 1099s, or report business earnings on IRS Form Schedule C.
  2. Your business is a non-profit or trade association.
  3. You will operate your business out of your home or a storefront.
  4. You will lease residential or commercial property.
