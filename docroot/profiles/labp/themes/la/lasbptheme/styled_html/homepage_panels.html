Welcome to the Beta Version of the LA Business Portal

Small businesses are essential to our city's economy. That's why the City of Los Angeles is developing this Portal, to help you plan, start, and grow your business. By September, you'll find rich resources and all the permits and licenses required for your specific business type.

Until then, try our Startup Guide to map out quickly what you need to do to register your new business and be compliant — it may be easier than you think! And browse our resources section to find out where you can get help.
